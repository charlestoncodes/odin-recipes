Charleston Chicken Tendies:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Eggs

Recipe Directions:

  1. Cut your chicken breasts into small and thin strips
  2. Rub the chicken strips with Olive Oil
  3. Crack 3 eggs into a bowl and vigourously beat them
  4. Soak the chicken tenders in the egg wash
  5. Add the desired amount of salt and pepper to the chicken strips
  6. Dip the chicken breasts into a bowl of breadcrumbs, coating the whole strip
  7. Put the chicken strips into the airfryer for 20 minutes, flipping them at the halfway mark
  8. The chicken strips should be a nice golden brown
  9. Serve with your choices of sauce and enjoy!
