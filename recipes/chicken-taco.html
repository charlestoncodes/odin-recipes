Chicken Tacos

Charleston Chicken Tacos:

Ingredients:

  • A package of chicken thighs
  • soft or hard shell tortillas (or both)!
  • guacamole
  • a red pepper
  • a large red onion
  • lettuce
  • a clove of garlic
  • cheese of choice
  • salt
  • pepper
  • peprika
  • olive oil

Recipe Directions:

  1. Prepare the chicken thighs by cuting them into small cubes.
  2. Rub the chicken thigh in olive oil and season it with salt, pepper and peprika to the desired amount
  3. Chop the onion and garlic
  4. Chop and prepare the lettuce and pepper
  5. Put a pan on medium heat with some oil and put in the onion and garlic
  6. Once the pan is hot and the oil is slightly bubbling, toss in your chicken and have it cook through (this usually takes 15-20 minutes while stirring ocassionally)
  7. Once the chicken is cooked and smelling delicious, assort your choice of hard or soft shell tortillas
  8. Place a spoonful of guacamole and a handfull of chopped peppers into the tortilla
  9. Finish by adding the desired amount of chicken into the tortilla
  10. Enjoy!
