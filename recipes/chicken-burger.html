Chareston Chicken Burger

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Pepper
  • Eggs

Recipe Directions:

  1. Rub the chicken breast with Olive Oil
  2. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and vigourously beat them
  3. Soak the chicken in the egg wash
  4. Add the desired amount of salt and pepper to the chicken
  5. Dip the chicken into a bowl of breadcrumbs, coating the whole breast
  6. Put the chicken strips into the airfryer for 15-20 minutes, flipping the chicken around 7 minutes
  7. The chicken should be a nice golden brown
  8. Toast your bun
  9. Add, lettuce, tomato and any other toppings to the bun
  10. Add ketchup and mayonaise to the bun
  11. Finally add the chicken to the bun and serve
  12. Enjoy!
